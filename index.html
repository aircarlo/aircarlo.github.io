Carlo Aironi, PhD

Machine Learning · Signal Processing · Deep Learning

Postdoctoral researcher at Università Politecnica delle Marche, Italy, focusing on deep learning for audio signal processing, including speech enhancement, packet loss concealment, music restoration, and clinical audio analysis for sleep apnea detection and dysarthria severity assessment. In parallel, my work extends to the application of machine learning techniques for predictive maintenance and operating condition recognition in industrial and access control systems.
